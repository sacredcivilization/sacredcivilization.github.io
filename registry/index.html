AKADEMIYA DARGAH GHAZI KOT  ·  KULLIYYAT AL-DIRASAT  ·  /registry/

Faculty of Studies

Two Kulliyyat — Six Research Papers — One Living Syllabus

{% include divider.html %}

The Akademiya Dargah Ghazi Kot organizes its deep-research studies across two Faculties — each tracing a distinct arc of the civilizational argument. This is not formal academic publishing in the institutional sense. It is a living syllabus: each paper takes a specific dimension of the SCRA's argument and develops it with primary source analysis, historical documentation, and direct engagement with contemporary civilizational dynamics.

The two Faculties are not parallel tracks — they are sequential: Kulliyyat al-Tarikh establishes what was transmitted and how it was stolen; Kulliyyat al-Ishraq maps where it survived, where it endures, and why it cannot ultimately be extinguished. Together they form the evidential spine of everything documented across the SCRA network.

Kulliyyat al-Tarikh

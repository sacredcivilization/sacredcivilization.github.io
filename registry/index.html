Research Studies

Six deep-research papers on the civilizational transmission framework

{% include divider.html %}

Wing III houses the six core research studies of the Sacred Civilization Research Archive. These are not formal academic monographs in the institutional sense — they are deep framework papers: each one takes a specific dimension of the SCRA's civilizational argument and develops it in full, with primary source analysis, historical documentation, and direct engagement with the contemporary implications of the framework.

Together they form the evidential spine of the SCRA argument: that civilizational knowledge moves through transmission corridors (Study I), that the most consequential transfer in the last millennium was a systematic theft (Study II), that the authentic Islamic transmission survived its own capture apparatus through an underground chain (Study III), that the eschatological framework maps the present civilizational dynamic with precision (Study IV), that the Indus Basin is the eastern anchor of that transmission (Study V), and that the chain from Idris to the present has never broken (Study VI).
